This item  provides two main benefits:

**Full Refund**: Recovers all XP and materials you invested in the character you use it on.

**Duplicate Conversion**: ONLY ON STANDARD CHARACTERS (not for Limited in the Standard pool or the starting SR), converts all extra copies of them into Standard "selectors," which you can then use to get or duplicate other Standard characters of your choice (not Limited in Standard).
This process works on STANDARD WHEELS too (not the Limited in the Standard pool).

It is used for re-allocating resources to build better teams.
