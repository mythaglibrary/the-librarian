This item is called **Origin Of Time**, which provides two main benefits:

**Full Refund**: Recovers all XP and materials you invested in the character you use it on.

**Duplicate Conversion**: Only for Standard Awakeners (not for Limited in the Standard pool or the starting SR Awakeners), converts all additional copies into Standard "selectors," which can subsequently be used to obtain or duplicate other Standard Awakeners of your choice (not Limited in Standard).

This process is also applicable to **Standard Wheels** (except the Limited in the Standard pool).

It is primarily obtained from the developers through special in-game mail during specific events, such as anniversaries or major updates. It is used for reallocating resources to build a better team.
